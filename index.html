About Faustus Helper

What is this tool?

You may or may not have encountered a situation where you needed to manually calculate market ratios below the auto-calculated ones to get the most out of your currency. Previously, you would need to pull out your calculator and work out the math. This tool does that all for you.

How to use?

Enter your currency that you are selling or buying, and enter your desired market ratio (hold down ALT on the market ratio in Faustus to see the competing rates).

Example

I want to buy some Divines with my 1263 Chaos Orbs. If the instant rate is 1 Divine for 164 Chaos, and I want it cheaper, I:
  • input 1263 in the tool
  • check the competing rates were at 159
  • it tells me "7 : 1113"
  • through Faustus, 7 Divines in "I Want" and 1113 Chaos in "I Have"
  • this guarantees a complete trade order with no partials
